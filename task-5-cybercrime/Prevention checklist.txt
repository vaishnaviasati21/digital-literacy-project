Stay Safe Online – Detailed Checklist for College Students




Account & Password Safety


* Use strong and unique passwords for each account (combine uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols)
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like email, banking, and social media
* Never share your passwords or OTPs with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or company


Safe Browsing & App Usage


* Avoid clicking on unknown links received via SMS, email, or social media messages
* Download apps only from trusted sources like official app stores
* Regularly update your phone and apps to protect against security vulnerabilities
* Check website URLs carefully before entering any personal information


Financial / UPI Safety


* Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone under any circumstances
* Always remember: UPI PIN is required only to send money, not to receive money
* Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown people or accept suspicious payment requests
* Verify the receiver’s name and details carefully before making any transaction


Device & Network Safety


* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking, payments, or logging into sensitive accounts
* Always log out from accounts when using shared or public devices
* Use screen lock (PIN, fingerprint, or face lock) on your devices


Responsible Online Behavior


* Do not share personal information like address, phone number, or ID details publicly
* Avoid posting sensitive or private content on social media
* Be cautious while interacting with unknown people online


Reporting Cybercrime (India)


* Report cyber fraud immediately at: cybercrime.gov.in
* Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24×7) for quick assistance
