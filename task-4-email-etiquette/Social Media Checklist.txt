Social Media Checklist for College Students




Do’s

* Think before posting anything online
* Protect your personal information (phone number, address, etc.)

Don’ts


* Don’t share fake news or unverified information
* Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
* Don’t overshare personal or sensitive details
* Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
* Don’t use social media during classes or study time excessively
